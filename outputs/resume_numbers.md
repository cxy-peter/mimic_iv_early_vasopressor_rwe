# Resume-ready numbers: MIMIC-IV early vasopressor RWE project

- Analytic cohort: N = 9,502 adult, first-ICU, sepsis-coded stays meeting 6-hour landmark eligibility.
- Treatment group: early vasopressor initiation within 0-6 hours after ICU admission, N = 3,772.
- Comparator group: no vasopressor initiation within 0-6 hours after ICU admission, N = 5,730. This group may include patients who receive vasopressors later than 6 hours or never receive vasopressors during the early window.
- Crude hospital mortality: 32.9% in early vasopressor group vs 25.2% in no-early-vasopressor group.
- Balance: 13/13 numeric baseline covariates had |SMD| < 0.1 after IPTW; max |SMD| improved from 0.288 to 0.046.
- AIPW-adjusted risk difference: 1.17 percentage points, 95% CI -0.52 to 2.87.

Suggested wording: observational treatment-strategy analysis, not causal proof; early vasopressor use is a severity signal in ICU data, so crude mortality should not be interpreted as treatment harm.
